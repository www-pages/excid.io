Governance, Risk, and Compliance

Cybersecurity is more important than ever. Connected products, cloud services, and software supply chains are exposed to constant risk, while customers, partners, and regulators increasingly expect security to be built into products from the beginning.

Security cannot be treated as an afterthought or a late-stage add-on. ExcID helps organizations identify gaps, assess product and operational risk, define governance processes, and implement security-by-design principles across the product lifecycle.

The Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) is a new EU regulation that defines essential cybersecurity requirements and vulnerability handling processes for products with digital elements. We help companies understand what the CRA means for their products and turn compliance obligations into practical engineering and risk management work.
