Cyber resilience services for products, platforms, and software teams

ExcID helps organizations turn security, compliance, and trust requirements into engineering practices that can be deployed, measured, and maintained.

Governance Risk Compliance

Connected products, cloud services, and software supply chains face constant attacks while the Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) raises the bar for secure-by-design products, vulnerability handling, updates, and lifecycle evidence.

ExcID helps teams scope CRA impact, assess gaps, define governance processes, and convert compliance obligations into concrete product and security work.

  • CRA readiness and product scoping
  • Risk management and compliance gap analysis
  • Security-by-design implementation support
  • Vulnerability handling and coordinated disclosure

Software Supply Chain Security

Modern software depends on code, packages, build systems, containers, signing keys, and delivery pipelines that often span many organizations. A single compromised dependency or release process can affect many downstream products.

ExcID designs and integrates traceability services that make software releases verifiable. ExcID can also help you implement secure software updates for your products

  • Software bill of materials workflows
  • Provenance records and keyless digital signatures
  • Integration with existing CI/CD services
  • Public and private transparency registries
  • Secure and resilient software updates

Identity and Access Management

Broken access control remains a critical security risk for interconnected systems. Organizations need precise authorization for users, services, devices, and agents without adding excessive operational overhead.

ExcID helps organizations adopt open-source identity solutions and implement fine-grained authorization models, including Relationship-Based Access Control.

  • Federated identification and delegated authentication
  • Relationship-Based Access Control (ReBAC)
  • Passkeys and digital wallet integration
  • Verifiable Credentials support
