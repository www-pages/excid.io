Senior IT Security R&D Engineer

Location: Athens/Hybrid

Role Description

As a Senior IT Security R&D Engineer you will:

  • Lead and manage IT security focused R&D projects, ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality solutions.
  • Adapt and implement technical solutions in the areas of Identity and Access Management, Software supply chain security, Traceability.
  • Implement technical and scientific deliverables.
  • Contribute to dissemination activities and scientific publications.

Qualifications

  • MSc or BSc degree in Information Technology, computer science, software engineering, or related fields.
  • Proven experience (2+ years) in EU-funded research projects.
  • Proven experience (2+ years) in IT security technologies, preferably in Identity and Access Management, Supply Chain Security, and Traceability.
  • Excellent written and spoken communication skills in English.
  • Ability to work with international teams and adapt quickly to new technologies and methodologies.

Solutions Architect

Location: Athens/Hybrid

Role Description

As Solutions Architect you will:

  • Design, lead and manage the development and deployment of company solutions, ensuring successful customer results.
  • Produce documentation, architectural diagrams, and design documents.
  • Adapt and implement C# technical solutions in Identity and Access Management, software supply chain security, and traceability of physical objects, documents, and processes.
  • Implement security best practices to safeguard the software from vulnerabilities and threats.
  • Integrate third-party user management systems.

Qualifications

  • MSc or BSc degree in Information Technology, computer science, software engineering, or related fields.
  • Proven experience (2+ years) as a Software Engineer, preferably working with C# and .NET.
  • Familiarity with identity and access management protocols, such as OAuth2 and OpenID Connect.
  • Excellent written and spoken communication skills in English.
  • Ability to work with international teams and adapt quickly to new technologies and methodologies.

Client Interaction and Business Development Representative

Location: Athens/Hybrid

Role Description

As a Client Interaction and Business Development Representative you will:

  • Engage with clients to understand their needs for security, digital identity, and access control solutions.
  • Conduct product demonstrations and presentations to prospective clients, highlighting the benefits of our solutions.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, IT, or a related field, or equivalent experience.
  • Experience in client interaction or customer service, preferably in a technology or security-related industry.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Comfortable with client presentations and public speaking.
  • Knowledge of security, digital identity, and access control solutions.
