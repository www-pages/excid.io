Senior IT Security R&D Engineer

Location: Athens/Hybrid

Role Description

As a Senior IT Security R&D Engineer you will:

  • Lead and manage IT security focused R&D projects, ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality solutions.
  • Adapt and implement technical solutions in the areas of Identity and Access Management, Software supply chain security, Traceability
  • Implement technical and scientific deliverables
  • Contribute to dissemination activities and scientific publications

Qualifications

  • MSc or BSc degree in Information Technology (IT), computer science, software engineering, or related fields
  • Proven experience (2+ years) in EU Funded research projects
  • Proven experience (2+ years) in IT security technologies, preferably in the areas of Identity and Access Management, and Supply Chain Security, and Traceability
  • Excellent written and spoken communication skills in English
  • Ability to work with international teams and adapt quickly to new technologies and methodologies

Solutions Architect

Location: Athens/Hybrid

Role Description

As Solutions Architect you will:

  • Produce documentation, architectural diagrams, and design documents
  • Adapt and implement C# technical solutions in the areas of Identity and Access Management, software supply chain security, and traceability of physical objects, documents, and processes.

  • Integrate 3rd party user management systems

Qualifications

  • MSc or BSc degree in Information Technology (IT), computer science, software engineering, or related fields
  • Proven experience (2+ years) Proven experience as a Software Engineer, preferably working with C# and .net
  • Familiarity with Identity and access management protocols, such as OAuth2 and OpenID connect.
  • Excellent communication skills in English (written and spoken)
  • Ability to work with international teams and to adapt quickly to new technologies and methodologies
